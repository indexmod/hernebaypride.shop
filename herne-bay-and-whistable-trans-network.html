Herne Bay and Whistable Trans Network

Herne Bay & Whitstable Trans Network is run and set up by a trans man.

A group for Trans and non binary community the meetings are in Herne bay and Whitstable but anyone from any area can attend it’s free. All are warmly welcome friends, ally, family, partners, of the trans community. the network group holds a once a month meeting in a safe environment to offer friendship , advice , tips , facts to date and more. There is no dress code come along in what you feel comfortable in it. its a safe space, no abuse will be tolerated.
